Everyone loves fresh kicks

The idea for TheShoeBox was born in 2017 when founder, Jeff Zalischi, wanted nice shoes but didn't have the time to shop for them. Being someone that is very active and on the go, he would contantly have to replace his shoes every couple months.

This was the start of TheShoeBox.
