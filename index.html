Send us your measurements

In order to garuntee these shoes are right for you.We request you measure your feet so we can provide you a shoe that fits amazing.

  • 1.Tape a piece of paper down to the floor. Trace an outline of your foot to measure
  • 2.Place your foot firmly on top of the piece of paper.
  • 3.Draw the outline of your foot all the way around.
  • 4.Mark the length and width of your foot on the paper.
  • 5.Measure the length of your foot. Use a tape measure or ruler to measure from the top to bottom.
  • 6.Measure the width of your foot.
